# Project Ashen 🌍
> **Because you cannot reverse what you refuse to measure.**

**Project Ashen** is an interactive, cinematic web platform built to bring clarity to the invisible carbon crisis. It transforms everyday habits into raw, visual data to help users understand and reduce their environmental shadow.

## Core Features
- **The Footprint Calculator:** An interactive, 3-step dynamic matrix that measures your daily carbon emissions across transit, diet, and consumption.
- **Real-Time Data Visualization:** Watch your choices instantly translate into total tons of CO₂ and the exact number of trees required to offset them.
- **"Hover-to-Heal" Prevention Grid:** A tactile, 3D flip-card system that reveals the biggest carbon polluters and provides direct, actionable solutions to stop them.
- **Cinematic Architecture:** Scroll-driven typography, fluid parallax elements, and hardware-accelerated animations create an immersive experience that feels more like a documentary than a calculator.
- **The Voices of Evidence:** Discover curated, hard-hitting facts and statistics highlighting the undeniable reality of climate change.
